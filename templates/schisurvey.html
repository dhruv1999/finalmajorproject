Screening Test


  1. I believe that others control what I think and feel.

  1. I hear or see things that others do not hear or see

  1. I feel it is very difficult for me to express myself in words that others can understand

  1. Do you ever hear or see things that others cannot?

  1. Do you struggle to trust that what you are thinking is real?

  1. Do you find it difficult to organize or keep track of your thinking?

  1. Do you feel that you are being tracked, followed, or watched at home or outside?

  1. I am treated unfairly because others are jealous of my special abilities

  1. I talk to another person or other people inside my head that nobody else can hear

  1. I have trouble speaking the words I want to say, or I am able to speak but other people have told me that what I say is incoherent.

  1. I have been diagnosed with a medical condition which I have been advised may affect my mood or behaviour
